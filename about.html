Our Mission

The mission of the FOSS Club at SIT Pune aligns with the broader goals of the Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) movement. It is centered on fostering a collaborative, innovative, and inclusive environment where students can learn, contribute, and grow through open-source principles.

Our Values

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork! By working together, we can achieve greater heights, share knowledge, and create innovative solutions that benefit everyone. Every contribution counts!

Innovation

Innovation is at the heart of what we do. We encourage creative thinking and experimentation, empowering our members to push boundaries and explore new ideas that can change the world.

Community

Our strength lies in our community. We foster an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome and valued. Together, we support each other’s growth and celebrate our diverse talents.

Transparency

Transparency builds trust. We promote open communication and share our processes openly, ensuring that everyone is informed and engaged in our journey towards success.
