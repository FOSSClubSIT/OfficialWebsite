Our Achievements & Milestones

Recognition & Awards

The FOSS Club has been officially recognized by the Symbiosis Institute of Technology as an innovative student-led initiative. Our projects have received acknowledgment from the FOSS United Foundation, marking our presence in the wider open-source community.

Our Android Auto implementation was featured in the College Tech Expo 2024, receiving the "Most Innovative Project" award among 50+ participating teams.

Technical Contributions

Club members have collectively contributed 100+ pull requests to various open-source projects, with a notable contribution to the OpenWRT Project for porting it to Archer A6 V4 routers.

We've successfully completed the implementation of 5 major projects including Custom Linux Distro, Android Auto, NAS Solutions, Reinforcement Learning Games, and our own FOSS Club Website.

Events & Community Impact

Since our formation in January 2024, we've organized 5+ technical workshops including the successful "FOSS in AI" workshop in April 2024 with over 50 participants.

The club co-hosted FOSSHACK 2024 with AIML SIT Pune, attracting 100+ participants and resulting in 20+ innovative projects over the 36-hour hackathon.
